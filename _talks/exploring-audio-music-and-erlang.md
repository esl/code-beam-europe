we don't "audio" when we think erlang. this talk
explores making sounds with erlang, in the most erlang native way possible. 

from sound generation, the issues we face with runtime system timing and performance, to making simple music with erlang.

there will be some live "coding", not so much of the system itself, but for sound generation.

**Key Takeaways:**

- audio is a stress test of the erlang system but one we can work through

**Target Audience:**

- anyone who appreicates beam and audio
