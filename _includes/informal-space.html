INFORMAL SPACE


This year we're also hosting a community-led informal space alongside the main programme. It may include all sorts of activities that would not exactly fit the talk format. Possible experiences you may find in this area will include: demos, panel discussions, slightly larger lightning talks, games, talks in languages other than English, hacking sessions and any other cool activity you can think of. If you have an idea of some informal activity you want to engage in or coordinate, let us know and we'll do our best to include it in the Informal Space.
