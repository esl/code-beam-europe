Aeternity Foundation Hackaton

This two-day hackathon pushes teams to collaborate in solving real-world challenges through automation, secure data processing, and scalable off-chain transactions. Whether online or in person, participants will work together to build impactful, blockchain-based projects using the latest decentralized technology. .

The Code Beam Æternity Hackathon invites developers, especially from the Erlang and Elixir communities, to create decentralized solutions using AI, oracles, state channels, and middleware on the Æternity blockchain. Participants will utilize Sophia, Æternity’s smart contract language, and explore the power of Elixir and Erlang in backend and middleware development for scalable, decentralized applications (dApps).
