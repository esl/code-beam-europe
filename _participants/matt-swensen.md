Matt Swensen is a software engineer at Jump, where he works on Phoenix/LiveView and frontend performance at scale. Previously, Matt was the first engineering hire at Rivet and helped grow the product through its acquisition by Zelis in 2026. Earlier in his career, he built and shipped products at Nuvi and Lucid. Matt enjoys contributing to open source and sharing practical lessons from maintaining production systems. When offline, he enjoys spending time with his wife and two sons, seeing movies or working on 3D printing projects.
